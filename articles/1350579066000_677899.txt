<p>Google&#8217;s earnings for Q3 were leaked early in a <a target="_blank" href="http://sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1288776/000119312512426975/d426664dex991.htm">SEC document</a>. Revenues are up 45 percent compared to Q3 2011 and now represents $14.10 billion. However, GAAP net income is down 20 percent to $2.18 billion. Non-GAAP earnings per share is at $6.53. Google fell short on expectations and stock is down today.</p>
<p>Analysts were <a target="_blank" href="http://investing.businessweek.com/research/stocks/earnings/earnings.asp?ticker=GOOG">expecting</a> $14.7 billion in revenue. Google missed by $600 million on those estimates.</p>
