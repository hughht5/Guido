<p>With Facebook narrowly beating expectations in its <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/10/23/facebook-q3-2012-financials/">third quarter earnings release</a>, the stock has popped up around 10% since the earnings dropped. <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/10/23/facebook-announces-monthly-active-users-were-at-1-01-billion-as-of-september-30th/">Monthly active users have steadily increased as well</a>, so all is happy in Facebook land.</p>
<p>Here&#8217;s the activity on Facebook&#8217;s stock as of this post:</p>

<p>As we know, <a target="_blank" href="http://www.marketwatch.com/story/facebook-expected-to-show-revenue-gains-2012-10-23"><br />
Wall Street projected</a> that the social network would report revenue of $1.23 billion and non-GAAP profit of 11 cents per share, so Facebook passed that mark.
