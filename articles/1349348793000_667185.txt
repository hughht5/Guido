<img width="100" height="70" src="http://tctechcrunch2011.files.wordpress.com/2012/07/facebooklogo.png?w=100&amp;h=70&amp;crop=1" class="attachment-tc-carousel-river-thumb wp-post-image" alt="facebooklogo" title="facebooklogo" style="float: left; margin: 0 10px 7px 0;" /><p>Social network Facebook officially hit the 1 billion active monthly user mark, the company <a target="_blank" href="http://newsroom.fb.com/News/One-Billion-People-on-Facebook-1c9.aspx">announced today</a>. In a post on the official Facebook blog, founder Mark Zuckerberg shared a note celebrating the milestone with a personal thank you to Facebook users.</p>
<p>Here it is in full:</p>
<blockquote><p>This morning, there are more than one billion people using Facebook actively each month.</p>
<p>If you&#8217;re reading this: thank you for giving me and my little team the honor of serving you.</p>
<p>Helping a billion people connect is amazing, humbling and by far the thing I am most proud of in my life.</p>
<p>I am committed to working every day to make Facebook better for you, and hopefully together one day we will be able to connect the rest of the world too.</p></blockquote>
