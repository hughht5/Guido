<img width="100" height="70" src="http://tctechcrunch2011.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/screen-shot-2012-10-02-at-9-23-19-am.png?w=100&amp;h=70&amp;crop=1" class="attachment-tc-carousel-river-thumb wp-post-image" alt="Mark Zuckerberg on Evening Urgant" title="Mark Zuckerberg on Evening Urgant" style="float: left; margin: 0 10px 7px 0;" /><p><a target="_blank" href="http://www.crunchbase.com/person/mark-zuckerberg">Mark Zuckerberg</a> is really going whole hog on this Russia thing.</p>
<p>The Facebook CEO is currently on <a target="_blank" href="http://news.cnet.com/8301-1023_3-57523568-93/zuckerberg-meets-with-russias-pm-medvedev/">a visit to Russia</a> as part of <a target="_blank" href="http://www.nytimes.com/2012/10/02/technology/zuckerberg-meets-with-medvedev-in-key-market.html?_r=0">a campaign to expand</a> his super-popular social network there. As part of his visit, he&#8217;s checked out Red Square and <a target="_blank" href="http://news.cnet.com/8301-1023_3-57523568-93/zuckerberg-meets-with-russias-pm-medvedev/">met with</a> prime minister Dmitry Medvedev.</p>
<p>And, in a remarkable show of &#8220;being a good sport,&#8221; Zuckerberg also appeared as a guest on <a target="_blank" href="http://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%92%D0%B5%D1%87%D0%B5%D1%80%D0%BD%D0%B8%D0%B9_%D0%A3%D1%80%D0%B3%D0%B0%D0%BD%D1%82">Evening Urgant</a>, a late-night show that is comparable to the United States&#8217; <a target="_blank" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Show_with_David_Letterman">Late Show With David Letterman</a> or <a target="_blank" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Tonight_Show_with_Jay_Leno">The Tonight Show With Jay Leno</a>. Actually, the host <a target="_blank" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ivan_Urgant">Ivan Urgant</a> is on the young and hip side, so it might be more similar to <a target="_blank" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Late_Night_with_Jimmy_Fallon">Jimmy Fallon</a>?</p>
<p>This is not Zuckerberg&#8217;s first time on late-night TV overall: He swung by <em>Saturday Night Live</em> <a target="_blank" href="http://www.nbc.com/saturday-night-live/video/jesse-eisenberg-monologue/1279517">last year when Jesse Eisenberg hosted</a>. But it appears to be his first time sitting down for an interview in the typical late-night talk show format.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s kind of amazing to see Zuck having fun while hamming it up a bit in front of a studio audience &#8212; how far we&#8217;ve come from that <a target="_blank" href="http://www.cbsnews.com/2100-18560_162-7108060.html">Leslie Stahl interview,</a> amirite?
