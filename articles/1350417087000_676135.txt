<p>Does Facebook&#8217;s first major video ad campaign confuse you? You know, that commercial that compares Facebook to chairs?</p>

<p>You&#8217;re not the only one. Not only <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/10/14/toilets-are-just-like-facebook-a-nsfw-video-parody-of-facebooks-chair-ad/">has there been a video send-off of the ad using toilets instead chairs</a>, there&#8217;s now a dead simple site that now lets you generate your own parody of one of the shots from the commercial.</p>
<p>Go on. Just go to <a target="_blank" href="http://drugs.arelikefacebook.com/">(Fill in the blank).arelikefacebook.com</a> and type in your own ideas. We have a few suggestions from TechCrunch staff below.</p>
