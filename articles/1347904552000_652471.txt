<img width="100" height="70" src="http://tctechcrunch2011.files.wordpress.com/2012/09/sigfigusatoday.jpg?w=100&amp;h=70&amp;crop=1" class="attachment-tc-carousel-river-thumb wp-post-image" alt="sigfigusatoday" title="sigfigusatoday" style="float: left; margin: 0 10px 7px 0;" /><p>Over the weekend, <em>USA Today</em> unveiled a <a target="_blank" href="http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2012/09/13/usa-today-undergoes-major_n_1881542.html">top-to-bottom redesign</a> as part of a new &#8220;<a target="_blank" href="http://www.adweek.com/news/advertising-branding/usa-today-redesigns-143665">digital-first</a>&#8221; strategy being undertaken by the 30-year-old newspaper.</p>
<p>As anyone <a target="_blank" href="http://www.crunchbase.com/person/mike-sha">who&#8217;s stayed in a hotel</a> probably knows, <em>USA Today</em>, which has the <a target="_blank" href="http://www.adweek.com/news/press/newspapers-see-gain-daily-sunday-circ-139974">second-largest circulation</a> of any daily print newspaper in the United States, is about as mainstream as it gets when it comes to distribution and mass appeal. </p>
<p>So it&#8217;s a big coup for <a target="_blank" href="http://www.sigfig.com">SigFig</a>, the relatively small <a target="_blank" href="http://www.crunchbase.com/company/sigfig">Silicon Valley startup</a> which just <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/05/01/sigfig-beta-launch/">launched its public beta in May</a>, to be powering the <a target="_blank" href="http://beta.usatoday.com/money/personal-finance/">new personal finance functions</a> for USAToday.com.</p>
<p>SigFig&#8217;s technology is helping to power <em>USA Today&#8217;s</em> redesigned stock page, online portfolio tracker, and upcoming new mobile apps. The company tells me it expects this could bring SigFig to &#8220;millions&#8221; more potential users.</p>
<p>The backbone for the new <em>USA Today</em> Money portfolio tracker is provided by SigFig and will contain all of SigFig&#8217;s existing features &#8212; the ability to track all investment accounts in one place, see interactive graphics that analyze portfolio risk and related fees, and more. The paper also plans to roll out <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/07/02/sigfig-iphone-android-apps/">SigFig-powered mobile apps</a> for its portfolio tracker for the iPhone, iPad, and Android platforms sometime in the coming week.</p>
