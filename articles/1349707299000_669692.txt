Hopefully, the budding startup scene of Detroit will be down for a party, too. </p>
<p>As part of our Northern Meetup Tour, during which we&#8217;ll hit up Toronto, Chicago, and of course Detroit, we&#8217;ll be on the hunt for the latest and greatest startups, the coolest investors, and the best possible conversation we can have. </p>
<p>Thanks to our sponsors, there&#8217;ll be plenty of food and beer at the famous <a target="_blank" href="http://www.hockeytowncafe.com/photo_gallery.asp?pagetitle=Photo%20Gallery">Hockeytown Cafe</a>, the shadow of Comerica Park, home of the Tigers. </p>
<p>But before the party even begins, we&#8217;ll be holding Office Hours in the area. Office Hours, if you are unfamiliar with the terms, are short 20 minute sessions of one-on-one time with TC writers. We&#8217;ll open up registration for these soon, so stay tuned. </p>
<p>In the meantime, check out how awesome <a target="_blank" href="http://www.hockeytowncafe.com/photo_gallery.asp?pagetitle=Photo%20Gallery">Hockeytown Cafe</a> is and get revved up for November 7. RSVP now for the free event <a target="_blank" href="http://techcrunchmeetupdetroit.eventbrite.com/">here</a>. </p>
