<img width="100" height="70" src="http://tctechcrunch2011.files.wordpress.com/2012/09/4911233302_34a1663d89_z.jpeg?w=100&amp;h=70&amp;crop=1" class="attachment-tc-carousel-river-thumb wp-post-image" alt="4911233302_34a1663d89_z" title="4911233302_34a1663d89_z" style="float: left; margin: 0 10px 7px 0;" /><p>Well here&#8217;s a Twitter <a target="_blank" href="http://storify.com/danlev/hilarious-twitter-bug-swaps-profile-pictures-and-b">bug that is kind of alarming</a>. Apparently, for some verified accounts, avatars were swapped out with other random users&#8217;.</p>
<p>Currently, the profile settings are disabled for all users, so you&#8217;re stuck with what you have now. Can you imagine trying to tweet and seeing some random Joe in your place? Yeah, weird.</p>

<blockquote class='twitter-tweet' lang='en'><p>We have temporarily disabled profile settings changes for all users. Our apologies for the inconvenience. Thank you for bearing with us.</p>&mdash; <br />&nbsp; (@Support) <a href='http://twitter.com/#!/Support/status/246767227142041601' data-datetime='2012-09-15T00:27:46+00:00'>September 15, 2012</a></blockquote></p>
