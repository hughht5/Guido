<p>Here&#8217;s what Yahoo! CEO Marissa Mayer had to say about the shutdown last week:</p>
<blockquote><p>This decision is part of our efforts to streamline operations and focus our resources on building a stronger global business that’s set up for long-term growth and success.</p></blockquote>
<p>The company is not finished doing business in Asia, <a target="_blank" href="http://ycorpblog.com/2012/10/18/korea-statement/">as it shared</a>:</p>
<blockquote><p>Yahoo! is committed to Asia. We have a solid business in APAC and see a lot of opportunity for growth across our content properties, communications services and ecommerce sites. We’ll continue to focus on building great experiences for the millions of people across the region who come to Yahoo! every day.</p></blockquote>
