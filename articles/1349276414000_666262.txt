<img width="100" height="48" src="http://tctechcrunch2011.files.wordpress.com/2012/10/mypunchbowl-1.png?w=100&amp;h=48&amp;crop=1" class="attachment-tc-carousel-river-thumb wp-post-image" alt="mypunchbowl-1" title="mypunchbowl-1" style="float: left; margin: 0 10px 7px 0;" /><p>Start to finish party planning site <a target="_blank" href="http://www.punchbowl.com/">Punchbowl</a> has raised $1.5 million in financing from Silicon Valley Bank. This brings Punchbowl&#8217;s total funding to $5.5 million.</p>
<p>As we&#8217;ve written in the past, Punchbowl&#8217;s platform allows users to create <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2010/10/04/punchbowl-debuts-digital-invitation-studio-invites/">beautiful online invitations</a>, <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2011/09/12/party-planning-platform-punchbowl-expands-beyond-invitations-to-digital-greeting-cards/">stationary</a> and track RSVPs. The startup also provides tools that let you organize an after party and even set a date, via an algorithm that recommends the best date for your party, set up gift registries, save-the-dates, message boards, add <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/05/11/punchbowl-amazon-mothers-day/">gift cards,</a> integrate Google Mapsâ€™ to display the location, and share comments, photos, and videos. The company just <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/09/25/digital-greeting-cards-site-punchbowl-turns-on-e-commerce-allows-you-to-shop-for-party-supplies/">launched</a> an e-commerce platform for party supplies.</p>
<p>Joan Nevins, the former CFO of Upromise, is joining the Punchbowl Board of Directors. Nevins joins Michael Waxman-Lenz, former GM of American Greetings; and Harry Lin, former GM of Evite.com on the company&#8217;s board.</p>
<p>The new funding will be used to expand marketing efforts and provide additional capital for Punchbowl&#8217;s eCommerce initiatives.</p>
