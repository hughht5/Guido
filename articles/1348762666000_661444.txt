<p>Apple has made a lot of stuff, like a lot. One enterprising fan, August Brandels, has created a three-and-a-half minute video that provides a good overview of most of that stuff, cut from various Apple commercials and promotional spots, and tied to a pulse-pounding soundtrack that actually really leaves you feeling fired up. The video isn&#8217;t chronological and it doesn&#8217;t include everything, but for long-time Apple fans, it&#8217;s a nice trip down memory lane that brings you right up to the present with the iPhone 5 and Retina MacBook Pro.</p>
