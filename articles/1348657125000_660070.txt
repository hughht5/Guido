<img width="100" height="70" src="http://tctechcrunch2011.files.wordpress.com/2012/08/samsung-expands-the-galaxy-s-iii-range-with_4.jpeg?w=100&amp;h=70&amp;crop=1" class="attachment-tc-carousel-river-thumb wp-post-image" alt="Samsung-Expands-the-GALAXY-S-III-Range-with_4" title="Samsung-Expands-the-GALAXY-S-III-Range-with_4" style="float: left; margin: 0 10px 7px 0;" /><p>Samsung&#8217;s mobile chief Shin Jong-kyun will meet with Google&#8217;s Eric Schmidt today to discuss Samsung&#8217;s patents battle with Apple, <a target="_blank" href="http://www.koreatimes.co.kr/www/news/tech/2012/09/133_120927.html">The Korea Times</a> is reporting.</p>
<p>&#8220;I will meet Google Executive Chairman Eric Schmidt on Thursday in Seoul,&#8221; Shin Jong-kyun is quoted as saying at a launch event held in Seoul for the unveiling of the Galaxy Note II.</p>
<p>The paper reports that Samsung &#8212; which uses Google&#8217;s Android OS to power smartphones and tablets &#8212; plans to pull Google into its patents battle with Apple.</p>
<p>The paper adds that Shin Jong-kyun declined to give further details about what would be discussed but he also said strategic meetings would take place with carriers in the U.S. next week.</p>
<p>We&#8217;ve reached out to Samsung for confirmation of the meeting and what will be discussed.</p>
