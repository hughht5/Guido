<img width="100" height="69" src="http://tctechcrunch2011.files.wordpress.com/2012/02/pivot3-scale-out-storage-for-video-surveillance-e28093-nvr-storage-video-surveillance-storage-and-scale-out-storage-for-video-appliance.jpeg?w=100&amp;h=69&amp;crop=1" class="attachment-tc-carousel-river-thumb wp-post-image" alt="pivot3-scale-out-storage-for-video-surveillance-e28093-nvr-storage-video-surveillance-storage-and-scale-out-storage-for-video-appliance" title="pivot3-scale-out-storage-for-video-surveillance-e28093-nvr-storage-video-surveillance-storage-and-scale-out-storage-for-video-appliance" style="float: left; margin: 0 10px 7px 0;" /><p><a target="_blank" href="http://pivot3.com/">Pivot3</a>, the Texas-based data storage company, and Samsung Electronics are entering a new partnership aimed at broadening the pair&#8217;s appeal to small and medium-sized enterprises wanting to deploy virtualized desktop infrastructure. Channel partners of the two companies can now offer a starter bundle to SMEs consisting of a Pivot3 <a target="_blank" href="http://pivot3.com/products/p3/">vSTAC VDI P Cubed</a> starter appliance (with pre-configured trial licenses) &#8212; capable of supporting more than 100 virtual desktops &#8211; and 10 Samsung thin-client <a target="_blank" href="http://www.samsung.com/au/consumer/pc-peripherals/monitor/cloud-display">Cloud Displays</a>.</p>
<p>&#8220;We’re delighted to partner with Samsung Electronics,&#8221; said Mike Holt, Director of Business Development at Pivot3 in a statement. &#8220;The combined offer is exactly what our joint EMEA distribution and channel partners have been asking for. This offer should really accelerate view deployments throughout our region.&#8221;</p>
<p>Back in February, <a href="http://techcrunch.com/2012/02/08/samsung-leads-23m-round-in-data-storage-hardware-company-pivot3/">Samsung Ventures led a new $23 million funding round in Pivot3</a> aimed at funding expansion of its product development, sales and marketing activities &#8212; and bringing the total funding in the company to <a target="_blank" href="http://www.crunchbase.com/company/pivot3">nearly $100 million</a>. At the time, Pivot3 also announced it was entering into a commercial partnership with Samsung Techwin, the video surveillance and defense subsidiary of Samsung. Brian Kang, managing director of Samsung Ventures America said of the investment: &#8220;We chose to invest in Pivot3 because we see them as a leading player in a large and rapidly growing space of strategic importance to our business.&#8221;</p>
<p>Commenting on the latest partnership, Andrew Gee, Business Development Director at Samsung Electronics, noted in a statement: &#8220;It extends our ability to support our customers, whether they are large enterprises or small and medium-sized Businesses.&#8221;</p>
