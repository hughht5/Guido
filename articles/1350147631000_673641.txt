<p>The Gillmor Gang — Danny Sullivan, John Borthwick, Kevin Marks, Keith Teare, and Steve Gillmor — bemoan the political dregs of the finally-engaged election before working our way back into the tech waters we&#8217;d abandoned. It doesn&#8217;t seem like we&#8217;ve yet connected the social realtime moment with the issues of the day, preferring instead to count the tweets and let the media avoid the substance.</p>
<p>But just when all hope seems crushed in a sea of SuperPAC money and Jobs nostalgia, we finally remember what one of us said about imagining the future. In turn, we make the effort to ask the interesting questions, merge the technology and the business moment, and otherwise till the ground for the next wave of innovation. Bring on the Mini.</p>
