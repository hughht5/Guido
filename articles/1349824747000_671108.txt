<p>Everyone&#8217;s favorite <a target="_blank" href="https://www.humblebundle.com">ebook bundle charity online sale thinger</a> is back and you have 13 hours to grab eight sci-fi books from folks like Neil Gaiman, John Scalzi, and the Craphound himself, Cory Doctorow.</p>
<p>This bundle has sold more than 18,000 units already and you can download the books in DRM-free epub, mobi, or PDF format. You can also send them directly to your Kindle. The bundle is worth $52, but the average donation has been $11 so far, which means you can receive the Scalzi book and a the Gaiman graphic novel for $12.</p>
<p>Books include <em>Pirate Cinema, Pump Six and Other Stories, Zoo City, Invasion: The Secret World Chronicle, Stranger Things Happen,</em> and <em>Magic for Beginners</em>.<br />
<br />
The sale supports the <a target="_blank" href="http://www.sfwa.org">Science Fiction Writers of America</a>, the <a target="_blank" href="https://www.eff.org/">EFF</a>, and <a target="_blank" href="http://www.childsplaycharity.org/">Child&#8217;s Play</a>. Plus a little (or a lot, your choice) goes to the authors.</p>
